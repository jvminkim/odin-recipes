One Pan Egg Toast

Description:

The One Pan Egg Toast is a delicious piece of egg toast made with only one pan! There are also two iterations of the toast as well.

Ingredients:

One-Pan Egg Toast

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 Slice Sandwich Bread
  • Few Shakes Salt
  • Few slices Butter

Cheese and Strawberry Jam

  • Few slices of cheddar cheese
  • Small scoop strawberry jam

Canadian Bacon and Swiss Cheese

  • Few Slices Canadian Bacon
  • Few Slice Swiss Cheese

Instructions:

Prep Ingredients:

  1. Cut one slice of bread in half.
  2. Whisk 2 eggs in a bowl. Beat it thoroughly. Season it with few sprinkles of Salt & Black Pepper.
  3. Dip the bread halves into the egg wash - coat only one side. Briefly set aside.

Make Breakfast Sandwich

  1. Place some butter into your frying pan. Place on low heat. Once butter melts and covers the entire pan, add-in the egg mixture.
  2. Immediately place-on the bread halves into the middle of the egg (egg-coated part facing up). Give it a slight nudge so the bread halves settles into the egg. Then do nothing - allow the egg to cook - don't touch it.
  3. After the egg has set, it should lift easily off the frying pan. Carefully flip it using a spatula. Then fold the eggs that are hanging over the bread inwards to make a rectangular shape.
  4. Place on your toppings of choice: Cheese & Jam, Ham & Cheese
  5. Carefully fold the sandwich in half. And give it a few minutes to cook, so that the cheese on the inside will melt.
  6. Next, place a small dab of butter into the pan. Give both sides about a 30-45 seconds in the butter to get a nice golden crispy top.
  7. Eat with your hands, or get a knife and fork! Bon Appetit ya'll!
